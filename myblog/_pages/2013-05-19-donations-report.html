Inspired by Jeff Kaufman's donation report, I also want to bring more transparency to my advocacy for utilitarianism and philanthropy. On 31 Dec 11, I first decided I should start donating, and started with $20. Then, in April, I decided to be "serious" about it, and give 10% of my pre-tax income. Of course, it's only a meager college kid's income, but everyone has to start somewhere. As of 28 Nov 12, I've been donating 20% of everything I earn pre-tax above $2400.
