Every month for all of 2013, I reviewed how my life was going. I also punctuated this with two six-month reviews, where I focused on longer-term goals. This year, I find both of those review cycles not working very well for me. While I still find reviewing each day, week, and month useful, I don't find it useful to write about them. Instead, I want to write in order to reflect upon my goals. However, I don't want to wait six months to do a six-month review. I think I have useful things to say now based on what I have collected. So one month is too fast. Six months is too slow. So now we're at a compromise where my only public self-reviews (and only lengthy ones -- the others all don't involve writing anymore) will be three month reviews. So now I'll do a little write up for each of my goals, do some analysis, and then come away with new goals for the next three months.
