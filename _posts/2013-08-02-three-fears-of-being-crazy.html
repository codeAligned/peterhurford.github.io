Some people might even consider these views "crazy". I'm a bit concerned about having these "crazy" ideas and talking about them openly. Sometimes, I don't talk about them in person out of these concerns, even when I think advocating for them would otherwise have high value. But what am I afraid of?

 

Actually Being Crazy

I suppose the first reason is that having "crazy" ideas might mean that I actually am crazy. After all, crazy people don't know they're crazy.
