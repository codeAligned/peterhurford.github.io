Diplomacy is a strange game where you have a map of World War I Europe and your goal is to capture up to 18 supply centers. However, each player has the same sized army in the beginning and there are no luck elements. All you can do is play off each other, forming and breaking alliances. No one wants to play it because its a really long game (I've heard games can take days to finish), but I want to play it because it sounds like a really exciting way to test game theory.
